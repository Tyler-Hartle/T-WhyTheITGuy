Short Video Format

Use this repeatable structure for TikTok, Shorts, Reels, or LinkedIn video.

  • “Today’s IT word is: RAM.”
  • “Plain English: RAM is your computer’s temporary desk space.”
  • “Why it matters: too little RAM can slow things down when many apps are open.”
  • “T‑Why tip: close unused tabs before blaming the whole computer.”

Carousel Format

Use each flashcard as a 4-slide learning post.

  • Slide 1: Word + icon.
  • Slide 2: Plain-English meaning.
  • Slide 3: Picture association.
  • Slide 4: Real-world example.

Start Here: Meet T-Why and Byte

The first video should make the mission clear: T-Why is the friendly IT guide, Byte is the funny helper, and every episode ends with one simple rule kids can remember.

The core safety rule

  • 1. Pause before you click.
  • 2. Ask why something wants your info.
  • 3. Ask a grown-up before trying it.

Built for safety-first learning

T-Why The IT Guy is designed to make digital safety simple, visual, and memorable for kids. The goal is to teach safe habits before kids face real online problems.

Simple, repeatable rules

Every lesson comes back to a rule kids can remember: pause, ask why, and ask a grown-up. This keeps the message consistent across videos, games, books, and classroom activities.
